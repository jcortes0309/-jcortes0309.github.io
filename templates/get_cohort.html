GetCohort

Live Project @ http://54.148.11.254

Description: GetCohort is a website designed to comprehensively showcase all of the skills and projects that members of a cohort have accomplished. By utilizing an easy-to-navigate layout that offers distinct functionality based on the type of user viewing the site, GetCohort provides a unique perspective on a cohort and the people that comprise it, from students to instructors and everyone in-between.

Technologies Used: HTML/CSS, Python, Flask, PostgreSQL and Bootstrap
